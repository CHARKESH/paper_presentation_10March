About Event

A paper presentation is a formal academic event where participants present their research ideas, innovative concepts, or technical findings. Participants prepare a research paper an one of the given topics and present it before a panel of judges using Powerpoint within the allotted time.

⚖️ Event Rules

1
Maximum 2 participants per paper/team.
2
Paper in IEEE format (max 10-15 pages). Include abstract (≤250 words), references, figures/tables.
3
Submit soft copy via Drive link. Bring hard copy & college ID on event day.
4
5-8 minutes presentation + 2-3 minutes Q&A.
5
Topics from suggested list . Original content only.
6
Judges' decision final. Time violations lead to mark deduction/disqualification.
