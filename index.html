About the Event

A paper presentation is a formal academic event where participants present their research ideas, innovative concepts, or technical findings. Participants prepare a research paper on one of the given topics and present it before a panel of judges using PowerPoint within the allotted time.

⚖️ Event Rules

1
Maximum 2 participants per paper/team.
2
Paper in IEEE format (max 10–15 pages). Include abstract (≤250 words), references, figures/tables.
3
Submit soft copy via Drive link. Bring hard copy & college ID on event day.
4
5–8 minutes presentation + 2–3 minutes Q&A.
5
Topics from the suggested list only. Original content required.
6
Judges' decision is final. Time violations lead to mark deduction or disqualification.
